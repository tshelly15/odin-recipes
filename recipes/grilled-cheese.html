Grilled Cheese

Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Either you're a deadbeat dad that went for cigarettes and never came back to your family, or you've needed to know how to make a grilled cheese. Whether you're making it for your kids or over a trashcan fire under a bridge, this recipe is bound to knock someone's socks off.

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices, White Bread
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Slices Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet to medium heat
  2. Butter one side of bread and place on skillet butter side down, add a slice of cheese
  3. Butter another slice of bread and place butter side up on top of cheese
  4. Cook until browned on one side, flip and repeat
  5. Repeat wit remaining ingredients
